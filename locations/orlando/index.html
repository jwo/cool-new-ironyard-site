City Highlights

In addition to its unrivaled visitor experience, Orlando is home to an incredible ecosystem of entrepreneurs and technology companies. In fact, technology is the region’s second largest industry with over 5,000 companies shaping the future of modeling and simulation, defense, digital media, and biotechnology. A thriving startup scene also has a strong foothold in Downtown Orlando, with one in five jobs in the area based in tech. Culturally, a hyperlocal focus is fostering a renewed vibrancy in the food and art scene. With Orlando’s weather, central location, and world-class attractions, this is the perfect place to live, work, and play. Some of our favorite things:

  • The Bungalower
  • Lake Eola
  • Downtown Credo
  • Orlando Tech
